About Us

Your Bridge to Vietnamese Law

Cao Minh Luat Law Office was established under registration certificate No. 41.01.1227/TP/ĐKHĐ issued by the Ho Chi Minh City Department of Justice, and is a member of the Ho Chi Minh City Bar Association.

Our team comprises experienced and dynamic lawyers who bring years of specialized expertise across multiple disciplines. We deliver high-quality legal services at competitive rates to individuals, organizations, and both domestic and international businesses operating in Vietnam.

Most of our members and associates are fluent in both Vietnamese and English. We maintain a network of expert consultants across various fields to support the resolution of technically complex matters.
